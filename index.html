Solar Street Lights

Street lights and security lights are used extensively to illuminate areas for safety and security. They are used in large numbers and consume enormous amount of electricity. In case of power cuts the security is either compromised or maintained at a very heavy cost in running on generators.treet and security lights can be converted to solar with obvious advantages. They not only provide uninterrupted lighting throughout the night but also reduce the electricity bills to zero.
Solar lights can be fixed on the electric poles, walls and display boards and give maintenance free lighting for years.

Integrated Lights

Integrated lights are nothing but a type security/lights. The advantage with the lights is that these are portable light weight and come as a single unit. No connections are required to be fixed. Just install the light where you want light and there will be light at night.

Solar Garden Lights

Solar Garden lights are small lights that are used to illuminate gardens, lawns and pathways. They are integrated solar lights which have a solar panel on top and a battery inbuilt. These get charged during the day time and automatically get switched on at night. They have a long life.
